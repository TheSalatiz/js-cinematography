Hi, I'm Juan.

Let's capture something beautiful together.

My story started back in Colombia. I didn’t have fancy gear or big connections, just a phone camera and a dream to capture moments that mattered. I eventually moved to the United States for college to study film, learning everything I could about how to tell a great story through a lens.

After I graduated, I packed my bags and moved to the brightest, busiest city in the world: Las Vegas! Here, I have had the amazing chance to film hundreds of beautiful weddings and huge events.

Vegas is fast, loud, and can be pretty crazy, but working here taught me how to stay calm and find solutions no matter what happens.

When you hire me for your wedding or event, my biggest goal is to make you feel relaxed. I don't want you to feel stiff or nervous in front of the camera. I will be your calm, quiet friend in the background, making sure we catch every single smile, tear, and dance move while you just enjoy your special day.

"Your job is to live in the moment. My job is to make sure you can remember it forever."

When I'm not holding my camera, you can usually find me hiking around Red Rock Canyon, trying out a new recipe in the kitchen, or relaxing at home with my favorite video games.
