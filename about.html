About Me

Filmmaker, Storyteller, Artist

My name is Juan Salazar. I’m a Colombian filmmaker and visual storyteller, but that’s just the surface.

Back home, my first camera was my phone. I didn’t have gear or connections, just the instinct to record moments that felt important.

When I moved to the US to study film and multimedia, everything changed. I learned the craft, the tools, the language of production, and worked with renown professionals. I became a cinematographer, an editor, a director, and a producer. But I’m still chasing the same feeling, trying to create stories that will stay with you.

For me, filmmaking is about emotions. It’s not just how it looks. It’s how it feels. I work with natural and artificial light, professional cameras, and determination to make something that you won't forget. Whether I’m shooting a personal short, a live event, or a branded story, my goal is to make the viewer stop for a second and feel seen.

I carry more than just a camera. I carry the responsibility of a dream that doesn’t just belong to me.
